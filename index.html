Tour Around Bacolod!

Bacolod, known as the "City of Smiles," has a rich history dating back to Spanish colonization. Famous for its MassKara Festival, it became a chartered city in 1938 and is now one of the Philippines' most vibrant cities.

Top Destinations in Bacolod

San Sebastian Cathedral

A historic cathedral known for its beautiful architecture and vibrant festivals.

The Ruins

An iconic mansion ruin offering a glimpse into the past and stunning photo opportunities.

Mambukal Mountain Resort

A nature resort featuring hot springs, waterfalls, and hiking trails.
